being display-only (which would violate the locked decision for fuzzy parity between tray and CLI).

alias matching cases.

    9. Alias score with prefix match: aliased("x", "myproject") — fuzzy_score("myp", repo) > fuzzy_score("myp", make_repo("x", "/myp/x", ...)) — alias prefix bonus applies
